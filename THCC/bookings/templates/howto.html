How to book online

Go to the calendar.

The rooms available for booking are shown along the left edge of the calendar. The times available are shown across the top.

You can drag the calendar left and right to move through the days.

To move further through the calendar, click "Next week" or "Next month" at the top right.

Look for an empty slot in the room you wish to book.

Click the mouse at the starting time for your booking, drag to the end time, and release the mouse.

Rooms can only be booked in half-hour increments.

Give your booking a title and description. If you don't do this, staff will not know what the booking is for and may reject it.

Click "Save".

Confirmation

Your booking will be sent to Centre Staff for approval. You will receive an email once the booking is approved or rejected.

Payment

Payment for room bookings can be done in person at the Centre or via Paypal.

You will receive a link to the THCC paypal account in the approval email.
